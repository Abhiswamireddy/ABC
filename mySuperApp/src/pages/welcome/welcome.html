WHY RIDERS HUT ?

RIDERS HUT is India's fastest growing Public Bike Sharing (PBS) system company in association with nextbike GmbH, Germany. RIDERS HUT tied up with Hyderabad Metro Rail to setup Bike Stations in Hyderabad Metro Stations. In all there will be 500 bike stations with inventory of 10,000 RIDERS HUT in Hyderabad. RIDERS HUT consortium which won the prestigious bid for setting up PBS system in New Delhi is aiming at covering most major cities in India soon.
